Common Questions
How does the AI shot analysis work? +
Record a video of your jump shot from a side angle. Our AI analyzes your biomechanics — knee bend, set point, elbow alignment, release arc — and gives you a form score from 0–100 with specific feedback on what to improve.
Can I cancel my subscription? +
Yes, you can cancel anytime. Go to Settings in the app or manage your subscription through the App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android). You'll keep access until the end of your billing period.
Why isn't my shot being detected? +
Make sure you're filming from the side (perpendicular to the shooter), your full body is visible, and there's only one person in the frame. Good lighting and a stable camera will also improve detection accuracy.
How accurate is the form score? +
Our AI uses MediaPipe pose detection to track body landmarks and analyze key biomechanical factors. While no system is perfect, the scoring is consistent — meaning your progress over time is reliably tracked even if individual scores have small margins of error.
